Pecel Ayam!

Give me a name of a person who doesn't like this food. I love this food so much, I missed this because it is only back in Indonesia. However, I will teach you how to make this.

Ingredients:

  • Chili
  • Chicken
  • Tempeh
  • 6 White Onions
  • 8 Red Onions
  • 1 Turmeric
  • 2 spoons of Salt
  • 1 tea-spoons of Pepper
  • 1 spoon of Coriander
  • 4 candlenuts
  • 1-2cm of peeled Ginger
  • 1-2cm of galangal
  • 1 or 2 the white parts of Lemongrasses
  • 2 or 3 Lemongrasses
  • 4 sheets of Bay Leafs
  • 5 sheets of Lime Leafs
  • Sugar
  • Boiled Water

Steps:

  1. Hazelnuts, red onion, garlic, ginger, galangal (hump) galangal, salt, pepper and granulated sugar until smooth.
  2. Coat the chicken and tempeh with the spices and put it in the pan.
  3. Pour the water until the ingredients are submerged and the lemongrass, bay leaf and lime leaves are submerged.
  4. Boil until the sauce and chicken are cooked drain.
  5. Fry chicken and tempeh until brown, serve with chili sauce and salad.
